Who We Are

Altura Design & Build is a residential remodeling and construction company serving the Bay Area. We were founded by tradespeople who grew frustrated watching homeowners get burned by contractors who overpromised and underdelivered — so we decided to do things differently.

We operate with a simple philosophy: treat every project like it's our own home. That means honest estimates, clean job sites, skilled workmanship, and communication that actually keeps you in the loop.

What We Believe

We believe your home is one of the most important investments you'll ever make. When you hire us, you're not just hiring a contractor — you're hiring a partner who takes that responsibility seriously.

We don't take on commercial work or stretch ourselves thin across too many projects. We stay focused on residential remodeling because that's where our passion and expertise live. Every project gets our full attention.

What We Offer

Our Services

Residential remodeling and construction — done right.

Bath & Kitchen Remodel

Your kitchen and bathrooms are the rooms that define how your home feels — and how much it's worth. We handle full-scope remodels from layout redesign through final finishes, bringing together design, plumbing, tile, cabinetry, and everything in between.

Whether you want a modest refresh or a complete transformation, we'll help you make the most of your space and budget.

  • Full gut and rebuild
  • Custom cabinetry and tile
  • Fixture and appliance upgrades
  • Layout optimization
  • Countertop installation (quartz, granite, butcher block)

ADU Construction

Accessory Dwelling Units are one of the best investments a Bay Area homeowner can make. Whether you're building a backyard cottage, converting a garage, or adding a junior ADU inside your existing home, Altura manages the entire process — permits included.

We understand California's ADU regulations and work proactively with local building departments so your project moves without unnecessary delays.

  • New detached ADU builds
  • Garage conversions
  • Junior ADUs (JADUs)
  • Permit management
  • Design-build turnkey delivery

Plumbing

Licensed plumbing services for residential remodels and standalone projects. From simple fixture swaps to full system reroutes, we bring the same standard of care to plumbing work that we bring to every other trade.

  • New fixture installation
  • Pipe reroutes for remodels
  • Water heater replacement
  • Leak repair and diagnostics
  • Full bathroom and kitchen rough-in

Unit Turnovers

Vacancy is lost revenue. We work quickly and efficiently to get rental units cleaned up, repaired, and move-in ready between tenants — without cutting corners on quality. Landlords and property managers trust us to turn units on tight timelines.

  • Painting (interior full units)
  • Flooring replacement or refinishing
  • Fixture and hardware upgrades
  • Minor drywall and carpentry
  • Cleaning coordination

Investor & Flipper Services

We understand the investor mindset because we've built relationships with people in the business. When you're flipping a property or renovating for resale, every week matters and every dollar counts. Altura delivers quality that shows at open house — on a schedule that respects your carrying costs.

  • Full-property flip renovations
  • Cosmetic and value-add upgrades
  • Fast-track timelines
  • Competitive volume pricing
  • Trusted repeat-client relationships
